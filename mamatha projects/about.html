About our school

The Learning DayCare is a progressive pre-school system that provides your child with modern education as per the British National curriculum incorporating the syllabus of CBSE and ICSE schools and learning of important characteristics of integrity and discipline that are ingrained within a broad canvas of social, moral and ethical values under one umbrella.

The most important development in a child is between the ages of one and five and this sense of self will probably stay with the child all his/her life. Our school creates an environment in which every child has an opportunity to develop varied talents. The child is encouraged to understand and master their environment, through experiences that are appropriate for his/her age and ability.
